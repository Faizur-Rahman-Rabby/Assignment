Ingredients:

Here is a simple recipe for a classic beef dish, beef stroganoff:

  • 1 lb. beef sirloin, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 8 oz. mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp. flour
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Egg noodles, cooked according to package instructions

Instructions:

  • Heat the olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced beef strips and cook until browned on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
  • Add the diced onion and sliced mushrooms to the same skillet and cook until the onions are translucent and the mushrooms have released their moisture, about 5-7 minutes.
  • Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook for another minute.
  • Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir until everything is coated. Cook for another minute or two, until the flour is lightly browned.
  • Gradually add the beef broth to the skillet, stirring constantly, until a thick sauce forms. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Reduce the heat to low and add the sour cream to the skillet, stirring until well combined.
  • Add the cooked beef strips back into the skillet and stir until everything is heated through.
  • Serve the beef stroganoff over cooked egg noodles.

Enjoy your homemade beef stroganoff!
